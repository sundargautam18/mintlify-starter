---

Annapurna Art ships original artworks worldwide with care, insurance, and dedicated customer support. Every order is fully insured and all customs fees and duties are included in your shipping cost — so your artwork arrives safely, with no unexpected charges at the door.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How long does delivery take?">
    Your order will be delivered within **30 business days** from the date of purchase. For made-to-order pieces, additional production time must be added to the regular delivery estimate. Delivery time may also vary depending on the country of origin and your destination country.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How can I track my order?">
    You will receive a tracking link by email **2–3 business days** after your purchase. Once available, you can also track your order directly from your account. Because Annapurna Art uses third-party delivery providers, the tracking experience may differ slightly from one order to another — some carriers may offer the option to schedule delivery at your convenience, though this cannot be guaranteed.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How much does shipping cost?">
    The shipping price is stated clearly at checkout before you confirm your order. Your shipping fee includes:

    - **Full insurance** — your order is 100% protected against damage or loss
    - **All customs fees, taxes, and document preparation** — no surprise charges on delivery
    - **A dedicated Annapurna Art customer care specialist** — available to assist you with any questions or issues throughout the delivery process
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What about taxes and customs?">
    All taxes and customs fees are already included in your shipping costs. In rare cases, the shipping company may contact you directly to request customs import fees — if this happens, please reach out to Annapurna Art support right away.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if my order is lost or arrives damaged?">
    If anything is wrong with your order, contact Annapurna Art immediately. Please photograph the packaging — preferably before opening it — and any visible damage to the artwork. The more photos you can provide, the easier it is to resolve the issue quickly.

    Your order is **100% insured**. If something is wrong, you are eligible for a full refund, or you may choose to have the artwork repaired or replaced at no additional charge.

    If you choose to return an artwork due to personal preference or dissatisfaction, all return-related costs are the responsibility of the buyer.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

Annapurna Art offers a 14-day return window from the date of delivery. If an artwork doesn't meet your expectations, you can request a return by following the process described below. Read the full policy carefully to understand eligibility requirements and the costs that may apply.

## Introduction

In case any artwork or product does not fit your expectations, or you simply changed your mind, you can ask for the return of your products. For the return of the product, please follow the steps below:

1. **Return Period** — If for any reason you are not completely satisfied, you can ask to return your product within 14 days ("Return Period") from the date of delivery. The return window will automatically be closed after the expiration of the Return Period.
2. Refunds will be processed back to the original form of payment once the gallery receives the artwork in the same state in which it was delivered to the customer and passes the inspection stage.
3. In cases where the return is requested due to change of mind or personal preference, or where no substantive reason is provided by the customer, the following amounts are strictly non-refundable: (i) original shipping and delivery charges, (ii) customs duties, import taxes and clearance fees, (iii) return shipping costs, including any applicable customs or re-import charges. In such cases the customer shall bear all return shipping and related charges.
4. All refunds will be subject to the exchange rate at the time of processing, and funds will be remitted in USD.
5. Additional shipping fees may apply for international returns.
6. Annapurna Art shall not be responsible for delays, losses, or additional charges arising due to (i) customs inspections, (ii) governmental actions or regulations, or (iii) incorrect or incomplete information provided by the customer.

## Non-Eligible Products

<Warning>
  Made-to-order products — such as commissioned artwork and custom or made-to-order design pieces — are **not eligible for return or refund**. The artwork will only be accepted for return if it is found to be damaged in transit. To initiate a return on the basis of transit damage, send an email to [info@annapurnaart.com](mailto:info@annapurnaart.com) along with photos and videos of the damaged artwork.
</Warning>

## How Does It Work?

1. To return an artwork or product, contact our customer support team at [info@annapurnaart.com](mailto:info@annapurnaart.com).
2. Our Customer Care specialists will inform you how to proceed with your return once your request has been received.
3. You will be responsible for repacking the artwork in its original condition for safe return. Only artwork received by the gallery in the same condition in which it was delivered will qualify for a refund.
4. You will receive return shipment documents with clear instructions.
5. If needed, Annapurna Art can locate restorers on your behalf, communicate with the artists, or organize your return shipment. However, all associated costs will be borne by you unless expressly agreed otherwise.
6. Once the artwork is received by the gallery, it will be inspected, and upon passing inspection, the refund will be processed.
7. The return timeline may vary and is dependent on: (i) international return shipping timelines, (ii) artwork inspection completion, (iii) banking and payment gateway processing times, or (iv) other unpredictable dependencies. As these factors are beyond Annapurna Art's control, a fixed refund timeline cannot be guaranteed.
8. If the artwork arrives damaged, contact [info@annapurnaart.com](mailto:info@annapurnaart.com) immediately, along with photos of the damaged artwork and its packaging.

<Note>
  Refund processing may take up to or beyond **45 days** from receipt of the returned artwork, depending on international shipping timelines, inspection completion, and banking or payment gateway processing times.
</Note>

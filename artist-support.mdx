Find answers to the most common questions artists have about selling on Annapurna Art — from account creation and artwork listings to payments, shipping, and exhibition opportunities.

## Getting Started

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I become an artist on Annapurna Art?">
    Create a free artist account on Annapurna Art and complete the verification process. Once approved, you gain access to your Artist Dashboard, where you can upload artworks, manage sales, and participate in exhibitions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long does account approval take?">
    Most accounts are reviewed and verified within 24 hours. If additional information is needed to complete your verification, our team will reach out to you directly.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What documents are required for verification?">
    You will need to provide a valid government-issued ID, relevant tax documentation, and your banking details for payment verification.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Artwork Listings

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I upload my artwork?">
    Log in to your Artist Dashboard and select **Add Artwork**. From there, upload your images, write a description, enter dimensions, set your pricing, and fill in any other relevant details before publishing.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What image formats are supported?">
    Annapurna Art supports high-quality JPG, JPEG, and PNG images. Make sure your photos are taken with clear lighting and accurately represent the colors of your artwork.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I edit or remove my artwork listings?">
    Yes. You can update artwork information, pricing, and availability, or remove a listing entirely, at any time through your Artist Dashboard.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I upload multiple images for one artwork?">
    Yes. You can upload up to 5 images per artwork listing, allowing you to showcase details, textures, framing, and different angles of your piece.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is the Make an Offer feature?">
    The Make an Offer feature allows collectors to submit purchase offers below your listed price. You can enable or disable this option per artwork when creating or editing a listing.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I respond to an offer?">
    Offers submitted by collectors appear in your Artist Dashboard. Review the proposed price and choose to accept or decline. If you accept, the order is automatically processed and payment is handled securely.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Team & Role Management

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Role Management?">
    Role Management lets you securely grant account access to team members, assistants, gallery managers, or studio staff — all while maintaining full control over your account.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What permissions can I assign?">
    You can assign permissions for tasks such as adding artworks, managing listings, accepting orders, tracking deliveries, and handling withdrawal requests.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I invite a team member?">
    Go to the Role Management section of your Artist Dashboard, enter your team member's email address, select a role, and assign the appropriate permissions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I remove access at any time?">
    Yes. You can modify permissions or remove a team member's access at any time through your Artist Dashboard.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Sales & Payments

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I know when my artwork has sold?">
    All orders and sales activity appear in your Artist Dashboard. You will be able to monitor new orders as they come in.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="When do I receive payment?">
    Payments are released 14 days after successful delivery of your artwork, provided no refund or dispute has been initiated by the collector.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are there any upfront fees to join?">
    No. Creating an account and listing your artworks on Annapurna Art is completely free.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What payment methods are available?">
    Artists receive payments through Stripe, a secure and trusted payment processing provider.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Shipping & Returns

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Who pays for shipping?">
    Shipping costs are covered by the collector. You are responsible for packaging your artworks safely in accordance with our packaging guidelines.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I track deliveries?">
    You can monitor delivery status and shipping updates directly through your Artist Dashboard.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if a buyer requests a return?">
    Return requests are managed through Annapurna Art in accordance with our Return Policy. You will be notified and guided through the process every step of the way.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Growth & Opportunities

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I participate in exhibitions?">
    Yes. Approved artists can apply for physical exhibitions, which are subject to curatorial review, and create virtual exhibitions directly through the Artist Dashboard.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How can I become a Featured Artist?">
    Featured Artist selection is based on portfolio quality, artistic merit, engagement, consistency, exhibition participation, and overall platform performance.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can international artists join?">
    Absolutely. Annapurna Art welcomes artists from around the world to join and sell on the platform.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

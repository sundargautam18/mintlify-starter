Founded in 2023, Annapurna Art is a contemporary art gallery and digital marketplace dedicated to connecting artists, collectors, interior designers, businesses, and art enthusiasts worldwide. We believe that every artwork deserves visibility, value, and appreciation — and that every collector deserves access to original works that genuinely inspire. Whether you are an artist looking to grow your career, a collector searching for your next treasured piece, or simply someone who loves the beauty of original art, Annapurna Art welcomes you into our creative community.

## Our Mission

At Annapurna Art, we showcase unique artworks that tell stories, preserve culture, and connect artists with audiences around the world. We are committed to making art accessible — supporting artists at every stage of their journey while helping collectors discover exceptional works that inspire and endure. Every artist and every artwork on our platform is treated with respect, ensuring that their creativity, value, and legacy are preserved for generations to come.

## Our Gallery

Annapurna Art's physical gallery is located in Radhe Radhe, Bhaktapur, Nepal — a culturally rich setting that reflects our deep commitment to art, heritage, and community. Alongside our physical space, our virtual exhibition platform lets you explore curated shows and featured artists from anywhere in the world, bringing the gallery experience directly to you. Both spaces are designed to celebrate original art and create meaningful connections between artists and the people who love their work.

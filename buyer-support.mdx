Find answers to common questions about discovering and purchasing original artworks on Annapurna Art — from browsing and making offers to payments, delivery, and returns.

## Finding & Selecting Art

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I find an artwork by a specific artist?">
    Use the search bar to look for your preferred artist. If the artist is not currently available on Annapurna Art, leave your contact details and we will notify you if they join the platform.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is Curated Art?">
    Curated Art features artworks selected by our expert curators based on artistic quality, storytelling, cultural significance, and social impact.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I request a custom artwork?">
    Yes. You can request custom commissions through the Annapurna Art platform. All communication, payments, and project management must remain within the platform.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How accurate are the artwork images?">
    We strive to accurately represent every artwork. Slight variations may occur due to screen settings and photography.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are artworks authentic?">
    Yes. All artworks are created by verified artists, and authenticity is guaranteed.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I submit an offer on an artwork?">
    Yes. Eligible artworks include a Make an Offer option that lets you propose an alternative purchase price. We encourage respectful offers that reflect the value of the artwork.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens after I submit my offer?">
    Once submitted, your offer is sent to the artist for review. Artists typically respond within 72 hours. Your payment method is only charged if the offer is accepted.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Buying & Payments

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I purchase an artwork?">
    Click **Add to Cart**, complete checkout, enter your delivery details, select a payment method, and confirm your purchase.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What payment methods do you accept?">
    We accept major credit and debit cards through Stripe, our secure payment processing provider.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you offer installment payments?">
    Yes. Contact our support team to discuss available payment plan options.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is an artwork reserved after purchase?">
    Yes. Once payment is completed, the artwork is reserved and removed from public availability.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Delivery & Shipping

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How long does delivery take?">
    Standard delivery takes up to 20 business days. Custom or commissioned works may need additional production time.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I track my order?">
    Yes. You will receive tracking information via email within 2–3 business days after your order has shipped.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Who pays for shipping and customs fees?">
    All applicable shipping costs, customs duties, import taxes, and related fees are calculated and displayed at checkout before you complete payment.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if my order is lost or damaged?">
    All shipments are insured. Contact us immediately with photos or videos of the issue, and we will arrange a repair, replacement, or refund where applicable.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Returns & Refunds

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I return an artwork if I am not satisfied?">
    Yes. You may request a return within 14 days of delivery, subject to our Return Policy.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="When will I receive my refund?">
    Approved refunds are processed according to our Return Policy and payment provider timelines.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Where can I find the full Return Policy?">
    Read the full Return Policy page in this documentation.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

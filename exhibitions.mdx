Annapurna Art exhibitions bring original artwork to life for collectors, art enthusiasts, and artists alike. Whether you visit our curated physical gallery in Bhaktapur, Nepal, or explore one of our immersive virtual exhibitions from anywhere in the world, every exhibition is designed to celebrate authentic creativity and support artistic growth. Both formats give artists a meaningful stage and give you — the viewer — a richer way to connect with the art and the people behind it.

<Tabs>

Each curated exhibition is accompanied by the artist's story, giving you a firsthand connection to the inspiration and craft behind every piece.

  <Tab title="Virtual Exhibitions">
    Annapurna Art's virtual exhibition platform lets you explore immersive online galleries from anywhere in the world. Each virtual exhibition is a fully customized digital space where artists present their work in a structured, professionally designed environment — no travel required, no geographical limits.

    ### How It Works for Artists

    Creating and publishing your virtual exhibition is straightforward through your Artist Dashboard:

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Sign Up and Get Approved">
        Register on Annapurna Art and complete account verification. Once approved, you gain full access to your Artist Dashboard.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Initiate Your Exhibition">
        Inside your dashboard, select **Exhibition** from the left menu and start building a new exhibition at any time.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select Artworks and Customize">
        Choose a minimum of five artworks to feature, then use the intuitive playground interface to personalize colors, layouts, and strokes to match your artistic vision.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Confirm and Publish">
        Review your exhibition one final time, confirm the setup, and publish it instantly to share your art with a global audience.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

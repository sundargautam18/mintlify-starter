This Privacy Policy describes how Annapurna Art Private Limited collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you access or use the Annapurna Art platform. By using the platform, you agree to the practices described here.

**Last Updated:** 30 December 2025

## 1. Introduction and Scope

1. This Privacy Policy governs the collection, use, storage, disclosure, and protection of Personal Information by Annapurna Art Private Limited, a company incorporated under the laws of Nepal, operating the digital marketplace known as Annapurna Art.

2. This Privacy Policy applies to all Users who access or use the Platform, regardless of their registration status.

3. By accessing or using the Platform, Users acknowledge that they have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to the Processing of Personal Information in accordance with its terms.

## 2. Definitions

1. "Platform" means the Annapurna Art website, mobile applications, and related digital services operated by Annapurna Art Private Limited.

2. "User" means any individual or entity that accesses or uses the Platform, including visitors, registered buyers, and Artists or Sellers.

3. "Artist" or "Seller" means any individual or entity that lists, promotes, or sells artwork or related services through the Platform.

4. "Personal Information" means any information relating to an identified or identifiable individual, including but not limited to names, contact details, and transaction history.

5. "Processing" means any operation or set of operations performed on Personal Information, such as collection, recording, organization, storage, adaptation, retrieval, consultation, use, disclosure, or deletion.

## 3. Categories of Personal Information Collected

1. The Platform collects Personal Information directly from Users when voluntarily provided through account registration, order placement, communications, or use of Platform features.

2. Collected Personal Information includes:

   - **Identity Data:** Full name, username, and profile photos.
   - **Contact Data:** Email address, telephone number, billing address, and delivery address.
   - **Transaction Data:** Details about payments to and from the User and other details of products or services purchased or sold on the Platform.
   - **Technical Data:** IP addresses, login data, browser type and version, and device identifiers collected automatically through the User's interaction with the Platform.

3. For Artists or Sellers, the Platform may collect additional Personal Information required for identity verification, tax compliance, and payment disbursement (such as bank account details or government-issued identification).

## 4. Use of Personal Information

1. Personal Information is Processed only for the following purposes:

   - **Service Delivery:** To manage registrations, facilitate transactions between buyers and Sellers, and provide customer support.
   - **Communication:** To send administrative updates, security alerts, and requested information.
   - **Legal Compliance:** To comply with applicable laws in Nepal, including tax reporting and anti-fraud mandates.
   - **Platform Integrity:** To detect, prevent, and address technical issues or fraudulent activities.

2. The Platform shall not use Personal Information for purposes incompatible with these original purposes or for unrelated commercial exploitation.

## 5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

1. The Platform utilizes cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance navigation, analyze Platform usage, and assist in security features.

2. Detailed information regarding these technologies is available in the Platform's separate [Cookie Policy](https://annapurnaart.com/footer/cookie-policy).

## 6. Disclosure of Personal Information

1. **Third-Party Service Providers:** The Platform may share Personal Information with trusted third-party providers who perform services on our behalf, such as payment gateways, logistics/delivery partners, and IT hosting services.

2. **Facilitating Sales:** When a User purchases artwork, the Platform will disclose the necessary Personal Information (such as name and delivery address) to the relevant Seller to facilitate fulfillment of the order.

3. **Legal Obligations:** Personal Information may be disclosed to government authorities, tax agencies, or law enforcement if required by a valid legal order or to comply with the laws of Nepal.

4. **Business Protection:** Disclosure may occur to enforce the Platform's Terms and Conditions or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Annapurna Art Private Limited, our Users, or others.

## 7. Data Retention

1. Personal Information is retained only for the duration necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in Section 4, or as required by statutory retention periods.

2. Account Information is retained for the duration of the User's active relationship with the Platform.

3. In certain circumstances, Personal Information may be anonymized for research or statistical purposes.

## 8. User Rights and Account Deletion

1. Users may request access, correction, or deletion of their Personal Information.

2. Upon a valid deletion request, Personal Information shall be deleted or anonymized unless retention is required by law.

## 9. Data Security

1. The Platform implements reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect Personal Information.

2. No system is completely secure, and Users acknowledge inherent risks.

## 10. Children's Privacy

1. The Platform is intended for adults and does not knowingly collect Personal Information from individuals under eighteen years of age.

## 11. Amendments

1. This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

2. Continued use of the Platform constitutes acceptance of updates.

## Uploading Your Artwork

<Steps>
  <Step title="Basic Information">
    Click **Add Artwork** from your Artist Dashboard to begin a new listing.

    Fill in the following details on this first page:

    - Upload the primary artwork image
    - Set the artwork's availability for sale
    - Artwork title
    - Artwork description
    - Listed price
    - Enable or disable **Make an Offer**
    - Date of creation

    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="What is Make an Offer?">
        Enabling the Make an Offer option lets collectors submit purchase offers below your listed price. Offers are typically expected to be within a reasonable range of the listed price. You can review each offer in your Artist Dashboard and choose to accept or decline it.
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Artwork Details">
    These details help collectors discover your artwork through search and filtering. Select the options that best describe your work:

    - Dominant color(s)
    - Artwork category
    - Subject
    - Style
    - Medium
    - Material
    - Tags

    <Tip>
      Accurate category and style selections significantly improve how collectors find your artwork in search results.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Additional Information">
    Provide additional details that collectors and our logistics team need to process and ship your artwork.

    **Artwork Information**
    - Multi-panel composition
    - Hanging hardware included
    - Frame included
    - Packaging method

    **Dimensions**

    Enter your artwork's dimensions in both:
    - Centimeters (cm)
    - Inches (in)

    **Shipping Information**
    - Artwork weight
    - Pickup location — used to calculate accurate shipping costs for collectors
  </Step>
  <Step title="Background Image & Publish">
    Upload a background image for the **Virtual Wall Preview**. This feature lets collectors visualize how your artwork looks in a real interior setting, helping them make confident purchasing decisions.

    Before you click publish, review all of the following:

    - Artwork title and description
    - Listed price
    - Dimensions and weight
    - Artwork category and style selections
    - Shipping details and pickup location

    Once everything looks correct, click **Publish Artwork** to make your listing visible to collectors on Annapurna Art.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Before You Publish

Annapurna Art is a curated online marketplace where artists like you can list original artworks and reach a global audience — completely free to join. Whether you're an emerging artist or an established name, the platform connects you with collectors, interior designers, and art enthusiasts from around the world, giving your work the visibility it deserves.

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Sign up for a free artist account at [artist.annapurnaart.com/signup](https://artist.annapurnaart.com/signup). Complete your artist profile to unlock your dashboard and access all selling tools.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Verification">
    Most accounts are reviewed and verified within 24 hours. To complete verification, provide a valid government-issued ID, relevant tax documentation, and your banking details for payment setup.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload Your Artwork">
    Once approved, log in to your Artist Dashboard and add artworks with high-quality images, pricing, dimensions, and descriptive details to help collectors find and evaluate your work.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reach Collectors Worldwide">
    Your published artwork becomes visible to collectors, interior designers, and art enthusiasts globally, giving you ongoing exposure to a growing international audience.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive Secure Payments">
    Payments are processed securely via Stripe and released 14 days after successful delivery, provided no refund or dispute has been initiated by the buyer.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Featured Artist Opportunities

Artists who maintain high-quality portfolios, engage consistently with the platform, and produce outstanding work may be selected for featured placements. Featured artists benefit from curator recommendations, priority exposure to collectors, and inclusion in special campaigns and promotions. Selection is based on portfolio quality, artistic merit, exhibition participation, platform engagement, and overall sales performance — so the more you invest in your presence on Annapurna Art, the greater your chances of being highlighted.

<Note>
  All communication, payments, and project management for custom commissions must remain within the Annapurna Art platform.
</Note>
